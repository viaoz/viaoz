Eco Candle Co. Case Study

Fall 2022
Computer Science 247 - Introduction to Usability
TAGS: UX ✿ UI ✿ Figma ✿ Wireframing

During the Fall 2022 semester, I had the opportunity to work with the local company Eco Candle Co.. I worked closely with 3 other classmates to create and conduct a case study to improve Eco Candle Co.'s website. Eco Candle Co. sells a variety of home based products and are well known their natural homemade soy candles.

Case Study Process

  1. Develop specifications document
  2. Create and distribute survey
  3. Assess survey data (Evaluation, Graphs, Raw Data)
  4. Conduct live interviews
  5. Design prototypes
  6. Develop case study evaluation
  7. Presentation

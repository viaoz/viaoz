Fox Valley Virtual Vet [In Progress!]

Fall 2023
Computer Science 346 | Final Project
TAGS: HTML ✿ CSS ✿ JavaScript ✿ Express ✿ EJS ✿ Replit ✿ Web Application Development ✿ Full Stack ✿ Cockroach DB

Problem

According to a research study published in 2020 on PubMed Central, only 67% of dog owners and 41% of cat owners received veterinary care from a clinic, hospital, or house call. In total, 21% of dogs and 52% of cats do not receive any preventative or routine care at all.

Background

As consumers, our overall preferences are changing. For example, 63% of us prefer to book appointments in some digital format. 2019-2021 marked an 81% increase in consumers stating that telehealth options were a driving force for patient loyalty. During the same time frame, telehealth appointments grew a staggering 338%. If we were to apply these trends in healthcare to veterinary, we’d find the latter extremely lacking in convenience and accessibility.

Online pet services are slowly growing, but are extremely limited in reach and provider ability. We believe that a veterinary health portal dedicated to the Fox Valley areas would bring a positive impact to the pet owner community by increasing service accessibility, thus raising the numbers of local households who bring their furry friends in for healthcare.

Objective

Develop an telehealth veterinary care portal specifically for pets in the Fox Valley area, enabling users to schedule online, in-person, or home-visit care for their furry companions.
